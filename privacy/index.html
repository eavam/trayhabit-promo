Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

TrayHabit ("the app") is built around a simple principle: your health data is yours. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data stored on your device

Everything you record in TrayHabit stays on your device:

  • Wear sessions and timer history
  • Your daily wear goal and aligner (tray) settings
  • Calendar records, statistics, streaks, and achievements

The app does not require an account, does not collect analytics, does not show ads, and does not send your tracking data to any server. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Purchases and subscriptions

TrayHabit offers an optional Pro subscription. Payments are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. To validate purchases and manage subscription status, the app uses RevenueCat, which receives a randomly generated anonymous identifier and purchase receipt information from Apple. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or any personal account. See the RevenueCat Privacy Policy for details.

Notifications

Reminders (for example, when your aligners have been out too long or when it's time to switch trays) are scheduled locally on your device. Notification permission is optional and can be changed anytime in iOS Settings.

Children's privacy

TrayHabit does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. All tracking data remains on the device.

Not medical advice

TrayHabit is a self-tracking tool. It does not provide medical advice — always follow the instructions of your orthodontist or dentist.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change (for example, if optional iCloud sync is added), this policy will be updated and the effective date revised before the change ships.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email trayhabit@nomadixapps.org.
